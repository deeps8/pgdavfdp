IQAC, PGDAV : The Organizer

PGDAV College is a constituent college of University of Delhi, having been established in 1957. Recently it celebrated its Diamond Jubilee wherein the President of India, Dr. Ram Nath Kovind, graced the occasion. The institution is guided by the DAV ethos combined with rigorous pedagogy aiming to prepare the disciples to be ethical leaders in the diverse and complex world. The college has been empowering the youth of India though educating and training them, equipping them with creative and critical thinking so that they develop their faculties of discovering, exploring, learning, and growing. For achieving all of this, and than some more, the college has well developed infrastructure – both hard and soft (the intellectual prowess). Thus the institute ensures that all the stakeholders enjoy intellectually engaging and fulfilling experience in an all rounded manner.

Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC), PGDAV College has been actively pursuing quality development, improvement, and sustenance goals right since its inception so as to deliver excellence in all its endeavors. In this background the cell has been internalized as an integral part of the institution’s systems and processes. The cell has calendar full of forthcoming events that will act as enablers and facilitators of quality management, especially in the sphere of dissemination and creation of knowledge.

The Programme : 7 Day FDP

It is incumbent upon all of us to be imbued with the spirit of scientific temper. For this we need to constantly imbibe knowledge as also create new one; this is especially imperative for all those who ‘educate’ others. We need to constantly sharpen the saw and use it to add to the existing base of knowledge and learning. Hence the need for this one week long Faculty Development Programme (FDP) on Research Methods for faculty members from across disciplines in Social Sciences, Humanities, and Commerce.

The FDP will focus on all the three dimensions of research, viz, material, methods, and procedure. It aims at imparting methodological insights to participants their equipping them with latest tools and techniques. Some of the key areas of the programme coverage would be sampling, use of research tools and techniques, research writing, IPR, citations, use of open sources, etc. The resource persons scheduled for the FDP are being drawn from a wide swathe of disciplines to cater to participants from all backgrounds. They have proven expertise in their areas of engagement, and constitute a truly eclectic lot. In all more than 15 experts will provide a fulfilling experience to the participants. An illustrative, but not exhaustive, and randomly chosen list includes luminaries such as Prof. Sanjay Kumar (CSDS), Prof. Ujjwal Kumar Singh (University of Delhi), Prof. Vivek Kumar (JNU), Prof. Ritesh Singh (Delhi School of Economics), Dr. Narain (FMS, DU), and many more.

Who should Attend

The participation is open to the faculty members, academicians, and research scholars in their respective fields. In addition, practitioners working in any functional areas and interested in research as also those professionals and executives from the corporate world who are engaged in data analytics and application would also gain by attending the programme.
